Important: Bird Flu Update (November 2022)

As per recent changes to the law coming into force on the 7th November 2022, and following recent Defra and APHA rules, all birds must be housed, and bird "gatherings" are prohibited. Moving of birds on or off premises will also be against the law.
Accordingly, we will no longer be able to accept any customer bookings for the forseeable future, and we will be contacting customers with existing bookings.
While we take this action regretfully, we started Sussex Hens with the primary goal of caring for chickens. Part of that care is in following the guidelines and now laws designed to protect birds and reduce the spread of diseases.
We encourage all happy hen herders to follow the laws, house their birds, and take extra precautions with biosecurity measures.

Please contact Jules on 07720207104 or email sussexhens@outlook.com for more guidance.

We are Sussex Hens

We set up Sussex Hens after getting our second batch of rescue hens (from the lovely people at Fresh Start For Hens) and wondering what to do with them if we were to go on holiday.
Now, we solve that problem for people like you and happily receive “guest” chickens, giving them the same care as we give our own. If you want to go away for a holiday or you simply need some time while moving house etc, we can happily look after your girls!
If you'd prefer to keep them home, we can come to you. See below for services and contact info.

Please contact Jules on 07720207104 or email sussexhens@outlook.com for more info. Thank you.
