Sussex Hens

Hello, we are Sussex Hens

We set up Sussex Hens in 2021 after getting our second lot of ex-battery rescue hens and wondering what to do with them if things ever return to 'normal' and we want to go on holiday. We have fully embraced the new lockdown lifestyle, feel very blessed with what we have and where we live and decided to solve that particular problem for others and stay put even when lockdown is a thing of the past! If you want to go away for a period of time, holiday or moving house, and would like us to look after your hens, we would be more than happy to. If you'd prefer to keep them home, we can come to you. Please see services and contact sections below for more information.

If you have any questions, please contact Jules on 07720207104 or email sussexhens@outlook.com. Thank you.

Our Services

Chicken Boarding

Overnight stay(s) £1.50

£1.50 per hen per night or a minimum charge of £8 per day or part day. Cost includes all food, water, bedding etc. We will feed your girls either layers pellets or crumble (as instructed by you) unless you need them on a particular feed which you will need to provide us with. If your hens are with us for a while, we can worm them if they are due. They will be given a treat in the afternoon of corn, mealworms and/or tasty veg. We can currently only house up to 8 hens but will be expanding soon. All hens have access to dry ground for dust bathing and to grass. Hens are safe within an electric fenced enclosure of ~35 square metres. The areas are sectioned within 2 electric fences to ensure they can remain separated at all times. If your hens' wings aren't clipped they will remain in the walk-in run.

Collection is possible. Please contact us for a quote.
